1. Avocado Toast with Egg

Whole-grain bread ke toasted slices par mashed avocado aur ek perfectly cooked egg ka combo aapke breakfast ko bana dega ek superhit start!

Pro Tip: Toast par chili flakes aur lime juice sprinkle karke extra tangy banaiye.

2. Quinoa Salad with Veggies

Cooked quinoa ko diced veggies ke saath mix karke ek zesty lemon-olive oil dressing add karein. Ye salad lunch ke liye perfect hai!

Health Hack: Extra protein ke liye boiled chickpeas ya tofu add karein.

3. Grilled Chicken with Steamed Broccoli

Chicken ko garlic aur herbs ke saath marinate karke grill karein aur steamed broccoli ke saath serve karein. Ek perfect protein-packed dinner!

Serving Idea: Broccoli ke upar grated cheese ya sesame seeds sprinkle karein for extra flavor.

4. Smoothie Bowl

Frozen fruits aur yogurt ko blend karke ek creamy smoothie banaiye aur fresh fruits, chia seeds, aur granola ke saath garnish karein.

Decor Tip: Bowl ko colorful banane ke liye alag-alag colors ke fruits use karein, jaise ki kiwi, strawberry aur blueberries.

5. Lentil Soup

Red lentils ke saath carrots, celery aur onions ko slow-cook karke ek hearty soup banaiye. Winter ke liye perfect comfort food!

Flavor Boost: Soup mein thoda lemon juice aur parsley garnish karein for a refreshing twist.
